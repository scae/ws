We're Getting Married!

December, 2014

Alexander + Sonya

After more than eight years together we are very happy to announce that we are getting married. Welcome to our wedding! This will be a celebration of our future together, which we look forward to enjoying with our friends and family.

About Us

Our Story

We met after Alexander watched some judo at the Sydney Olympics and thought, "I can do that!", and joined the judo club at UNSW where Sonya was already an up and coming young competitor. For several years we were friends at judo training and at university (where we both studied) before we "found each other" and really began this journey together.

There were some difficult times for us along the way, with long periods where Sonya's judo career took her overseas for months at a time but our strong friendship and mutual support at the core of our relationship has always seen us through.

Many years and sport-related injuries later, Alexander realises that judo turns out to be a lot harder than it looks but it was all worth it because now we both get to marry our best friend in the presence of all our other friends and family.

Dress Code

Suggested dress code is Cocktail / Lounge Suit. We want our guests to be comfortable so please take this is as a guide only and ditch the ties if it's a hot day.

Wet Weather

The ceremony is planned for outdoors in the courtyard. If it is raining it will be moved under cover.
In case of wet weather you may need an umbrella to get to and from your transportation.

Accommodation

If you are travelling from overseas or interstate and need a hotel, North Sydney is a short taxi ride and has a number to choose from. Otherwise, Sydney CBD is the most accessible location.

Travel

By Car:
There is plenty of parking on site. Disabled parking is available with a valid RTA permit.
By Taxi:
If you aren't driving, a taxi from the CBD will take 20-30 minutes depending on traffic. The venue can book a taxi for you at the end of the night.
By Bus:
Route 244 departing from Wynyard Station in Carrington St will drop you off on Middle Head Road, near Suakin Drive. From there it is a 500m walk down Suakin Drive.
